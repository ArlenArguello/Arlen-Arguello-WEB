1. Improve navigation in communication channels. (Contact us, find us and search)


2. Improve access to BANCACLICK.

Achieved goals:

2. The exchange rate was added because it is information that customers require in a visible way.


3. BANCACLICK was redesigned eliminating the three options it had because they led to different forms leading to the same site, which generated confusion in customers.

4. A right sidebar was added with access buttons to those corresponding to the communication channels, achieving the objective of a friendly interface.
